Callie Ingram

Callie Ingram is an English PhD Candidate at the University at Buffalo, with research interests in twentieth and twenty-first century American literature, contemporary fiction, experimental women writers, narrative ethics, and theories of reading. She received her BA from Ursinus College and her MA from the University of Delaware. Her scholarly work has been published in journals such as Critique: Studies in Contemporary Fiction and American Book Review, and her poetry can be found in P-Queue, Always Crashing, and elsewhere.
