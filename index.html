Callie Ingram

Callie Ingram is an Assistant Professor of English at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology with research interests in twentieth and twenty-first century American literature, contemporary fiction, experimental women writers, narrative ethics, and theories of reading. She received her BA from Ursinus College, MA from the University of Delaware, and PhD from the University at Buffalo. Her scholarly work has been published in journals such as Critique: Studies in Contemporary Fiction and American Book Review, and her poetry can be found in P-Queue, Always Crashing, and elsewhere.
